Memberships

Free

Becoming a Free Member of the Chamber of Commerce is simple and straightforward. Just sign up on our website or visit our customer service center to get started. Enjoy basic membership benefits and stay connected with the latest updates and events.

Bronze

To achieve Bronze Membership status, you need to accumulate purchases totaling more than $500 in the past six months within the program's eligible categories. Bronze Members enjoy enhanced benefits, including access to exclusive events and networking opportunities.

Silver

To become a Silver Member, you need to accumulate purchases totaling more than $1,000 in the past six months within the program's eligible categories. Silver Members receive additional perks, such as premium event invitations and special discounts on services and products.

Gold

To reach Gold Membership, spend over $1,500 in eligible categories in the past six months. Enjoy VIP event access, exclusive networking, and significant discounts.
